Welcome to Bulbul

Speak in any language. Type anywhere.

1
Click into any app Open Notepad, VS Code, Chrome, WhatsApp Web — anything with a text field. Click where you want text to appear.
2
Press Ctrl + Shift + V to start The tray icon will start blinking 🔴. Speak naturally in English, Hindi, Tamil — or mix them.
3
Watch text appear Your speech is transcribed and typed automatically. You can switch windows while speaking.
4
Press Ctrl + Shift + V to stop The blinking stops. Bulbul keeps running silently in the system tray — ready whenever you need it.

Bulbul runs in your system tray (bottom-right corner).
Right-click the tray icon to quit.
